About PolEval

PolEval is a SemEval-inspired evaluation campaign for natural language processing tools for Polish. Submitted tools compete against one another within certain tasks selected by organisers, using available data and are evaluated according to pre-established procedures.

Prizes

Thanks to our sponsors we are able to offer prizes for the winners of the PolEval competition who will agree to open-source and submit the source code of their methods and the machine learning models used. The authors of the best performing methods in each of the tasks (and subtasks) will be awarded a prize of 750 PLN. The winning methods will be chosen according to the rules described on the Evaluation and Tasks page.

Publication

PolEval 2017 papers were presented during the special session organized at the 8th Language & Technology Conference: Human Language Technologies as a Challenge for Computer Science and Linguistics (LTC 2017) in Poznań on November 19, 2017. 

All system descriptions following the LTC 2017 review process have been published in the conference proceedings. You can find the links to individual papers at the conference website.

Extended versions of best papers from each main track will be recommended for publication in the post-conference Springer's LNAI volume (subject to additional rounds of reviews).
